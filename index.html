Bio

I am now a third-year Ph.D. candidate advised by Prof. Qun Huang. at School of Computer Science, Peking University. I got my M.S. degree in Computer Architecture from Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ICT-CAS) in 2020, advised by Prof. Qun Huang and Prof. Yungang Bao. I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science from Beijing Forestry University in 2017. My research mainly focuses on network measurement, programmable network, and high-performance network.
